Student Opportunities

Students can participate in a variety of experiences throughout the year to develop a strong sense of civic identity and a deep commitment to civic engagement.  From short-term opportunities that introduce students to our local community and public issues, to long-term commitments for students looking for immersive civic experiences, Civic Engagement Initiatives supports each student's civic development!
